Python (genus)

Python, from the Greek word, is a genus of nonvenomous Pythonidae found in Africa and Asia. Until recently, seven extant species were recognised; however, three subspecies have been promoted and a new species recognised. A member of this genus, P. reticulatus, is among the longest snake species and extant reptiles in the world. More at Wikipedia
